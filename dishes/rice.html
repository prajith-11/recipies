Rice

A staple dish across the southern part of India. It is grown throughout the year and consumed by the people everyday.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Uncooked rice
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Take 3 table-glasses of rice in a rice cooker
  2. Wash it 2-3 times, making sure that none of the rice gets wasted
  3. Now fill the cooker with water, upto 1/3rd height above rice level
  4. Turn on the gas-stove and leave it until 3-4 whistles
  5. Turn off the stove and let the cooker cool down
  6. Once the cooker cools down, open the lid and your cooked rice is ready to be served
