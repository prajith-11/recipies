Rice

Rice is a staple dish in southern part of India. It is grown almost everyday throughout the year by the people.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Uncooked rice
  • Salt

Steps

  • Take 3 table-glasses of rice in a rice cooker
  • Wash it 2-3 times, making sure that none of the rice gets wasted
  • Now fill the cooker with water, upto 1/3rd height above rice level
  • Turn on the gas-stove and leave it until 3-4 whistles
  • Turn off the stove and let the cooker cool down
  • Once the cooker cools down, open the lid and your cooked rice is ready to be served
