Tea

Tea - a hot beverage consumed across major parts of the world, especially India. It is a refreshing drink usually consumed during the evenings along with light snacks.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Milk
  • Tea powder
  • Sugar
  • Ginger

Steps

  1. Pour 2 glasses of water into a container and let it boil
  2. While boiling, put 3 spoons of sugar and 3 spoons of tea powder into it
  3. Let it boil for sometime, then add small pieces of ginger for taste
  4. Pour about 2 glasses of milk and let it boil
  5. Take a mesh and filter out the tea into 4-5 cups
  6. Your tea is now ready to be served
